Marketing Research

Entrepreneurs make decisions every minute, and every such decision should be backed by data to minimize the chances of failure. This is where market research steps in.

Market research process is the practice that involves identifying a problem in a business that is to be dealt with finding a solution to that problem by getting consumer feedback, and analysing the feedback to decide what is to be done to solve the issue in the most effective way possible.
